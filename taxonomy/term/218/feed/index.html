With Intel making a <em>huge</em> presence volunteering to lead the huge troves of student groups, eagerly awaiting a glimpse at the wonders awaiting inside the gates.</p> <p><a href="http://sylviashow.com/sites/default/files/post-images/Dadmin/IMG_1975.JPG" class="yoxview_thumbnail"><img class="post-img-c" typeof="foaf:Image" src="http://sylviashow.com/sites/default/files/styles/blog_large/public/post-images/Dadmin/IMG_1975.JPG?itok=o1KYO_XV" alt="Sylvia signing magazines" title="Sylvia signed almost 100 magazines for eager students as they exited" /></a></p> <p>We found our way to the exit accidentally, and Sylvia hopped on board to sign about 100 of the special "School's Out" MAKE Magazines being handed out to students as they leave! Sylvia had a blast playing celebrity for the moment, then it has back to the cool stuff.</p> <p>What few makers were setup, still had lots to show off. From <a href="http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/joylabs/makey-makey-an-invention-kit-for-everyone?ref=live">a kickstarter</a> we'd only seen last week was <a href="http://makeymakey.com/">Makey Makey</a>, an instant touch interface for makers to turn anything conductive into a keyboard key. With beach balls and bananas kids were amazed at how easy it was to turn every day objects into interfaces with just alligator clips and a simple little board.</p> <p><a href="http://sylviashow.com/sites/default/files/post-images/Dadmin/IMG_1986.JPG" class="yoxview_thumbnail"><img class="post-img-c" typeof="foaf:Image" src="http://sylviashow.com/sites/default/files/styles/blog_large/public/post-images/Dadmin/IMG_1986.JPG?itok=6aNsZugU" alt="Sylvia plays the banana piano via Makey Makey" title="You could actually play a tune on these Makey Makey powered piano bananas, just don&#039;t press too hard. " /></a></p> <p>After the students had all left, it was time for the teacher education portion of the day, and with us squarely in both categories, we stayed for the fun. <a Stager</a>, <a href="https://twitter.com/sdavee">Steve Davee</a> and many more big personalities in real world hands on education.</p> <p><a
