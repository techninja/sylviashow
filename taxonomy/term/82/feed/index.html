It all averaged out).</aside></li> <li><strong>Entertainment: </strong><em>$125</em> <aside>(American natural history museum!)</aside></li> </ul><h4>That puts our New York Adventure grand total at only <em>$4,052!</em></h4> <p>Our complete take away (after fees) from IndieGoGo was $5,074, less that total, leaves us with $1,022. We’re estimating perk fulfillment to be about $200 in shipping and other costs, leaving us with $822 to help make the next few episodes <em>absolutely <strong>super-awesome!</strong></em> </p></div> <p>To celebrate, we’re going to start <em><strong>planning</strong></em>! (instead of the 15th like last year!), also, we’re putting up <strong>all</strong> the photos we can of our various adventures into Google+ albums here, check em out!
