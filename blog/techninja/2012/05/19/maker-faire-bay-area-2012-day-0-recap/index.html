Maker Faire Bay Area 2012: Day 0 Recap

Submitted by TechNinja on May 19, 2012 - 12:30am

(Continued from Maker Faire: Education Day) The Friday before Maker Faire weekend, open to makers and their families to set up, was filled with a teaser of the awesome things to come at the faire.

Our day started with a quick trip to HSC Electronics surplus, an incredible and seemingly endless smattering of amazing odds, ends and components. Then after a nap we crashed the Maker Faire party and got to sneak around as everyone hastily prepared and setup for the final time before the big event.

And to think, that's only beginning of this year's marvelous Maker Faire.
